02 — AboutWho I am

About

Most people learning AI stop at calling the API. I want the layer underneath — why a retrieval pipeline returns the wrong chunk, why an agent loops, what the maths is actually doing. That's the part I keep chasing.

I'm a Data Science & AI student at Leiden University, also in the Honours Programme. Right now I'm interning remotely at flyrank.ai, working on retrieval pipelines, after a first internship at DecodeLabs. Alongside coursework I run a structured AI-engineering track of my own — building an LLM from scratch, RAG over my own notes, and a few smaller things that exist mainly because I wanted to know how they worked.

Everything on this page is built rather than described. That's deliberate.

CurrentlyUpdated Jul 2026

Interning at flyrank.ai on retrieval pipelines. Working through the AI-engineering roadmap at an hour a day — currently on transformers from first principles. Rebuilding this site.
